Appendix — Glossary

  • BAA (Business Associate Agreement): a contract required under HIPAA with any vendor that handles patient data on your behalf.
  • PHI (Protected Health Information): patient data protected under HIPAA.
  • MFA (Multi-Factor Authentication): a second verification step beyond a password.
  • Least privilege: giving each person only the access they need to do their job.
